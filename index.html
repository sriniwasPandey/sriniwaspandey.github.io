About Me

I am Sriniwas Pandey, a Lecturer in Computer Science at Binghamton University. Holding a Ph.D. in Systems Science from Binghamton University, my research focuses on Complex Systems, Social Behavior Dynamics, and Computational Social Science.

Balancing work and life is integral to my philosophy, which is reflected in both my research and teaching methodologies. I continuously strive for personal and professional growth, embracing evolution and adaptability.
